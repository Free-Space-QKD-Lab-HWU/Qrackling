Description

Qrackling is a flexible objec-oriented MATLAB toolbox for simulating quantum key distribution (QKD) between satellites and ground stations. It is easily extended and can be used to replicate satellite QKD presented in the literature as well as explore the features present within them. Qrackling has been developed at Heriot-Watt University in the Free-Space QKD group to aid in the design and development of the Hub Optical Ground Station (HOGS).
